Using this Site

If you use assistive technology (Braille reader, screen reader, or TTY) and the format of any material on this official Government of Star Land site interferes with your ability to access information, please contact us.

To enable us to respond in a manner most helpful to you, please log into your secure Star Land Account (SLA) and indicate the nature of your accessibility problem. Please include the Web address of the accessibility problem.

Users who need accessibility assistance can also contact us by phone through the Federal Star Land Information Service at 1-800-180-1104 for a voice prompt.

You can reach a live operator at any time by pressing zero (0) on your Star Land comm panel just inside your door, by calling SL0 on your Smartphone or by simply picking up any blue courtesy phone located at every section number.
